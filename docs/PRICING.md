# Pricing & Plans

Indicative plans. Actual pricing may vary by contract, region, and data licensing.

## Seat Plans (includes dashboard)
- Starter: 3 seats, basic connectors, 100k articles/month, $199/mo
- Growth: 10 seats, standard connectors, 1M articles/month, $699/mo
- Enterprise: custom seats, premium connectors, custom limits, contact sales

## Usage‑Based API Plans
- Developer: no seats, 50k articles/month included, $0.001 per article overage
- Pro: 300k included, $0.0007 overage
- Enterprise: committed use discounts and SLAs

## Add‑Ons
- Premium connectors (e.g., Dow Jones, Bloomberg): pass‑through or custom
- Historical backfill: $X per 10k records (tiered)
- Dedicated throughput / private cluster

## Notes
- All times handled as UTC; data licensing restrictions apply.
- Overages are billed monthly; rate limits may be temporarily increased upon request.
- Webhooks/streaming included in all plans.
